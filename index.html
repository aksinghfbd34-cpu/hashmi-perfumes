Our Heritage & Craft

Formulated from Tradition,
Nurtured for Elegance

At Hashmi Perfumes, we preserve the ancient art of Indian perfumery. Our attars (Itra) are distilled using traditional methods, utilizing only natural ingredients like precious woods, flowers, roots, and spices. We firmly believe that a true attar represents an intimate connection between nature and the soul.

Every single bottle is 100% alcohol-free, concentrated, and hand-filled in Patencheru, Telangana. Each drop provides a rich fragrance journey that evolves on your skin, projecting warmth and clean energy for up to 24 hours.
